About Casio

Casio is dedicated to delivering innovative and reliable products that enhance the lives of our customers. From watches to calculators, we focus on quality and precision.


Our History

Casio was founded in April 1946 by Tadao Kashio, an engineer specializing in fabrication technology. Casio's first major product was the Yubiwa Pipe, a finger ring that would hold a cigarette, allowing a smoker to smoke the cigarette down to its nub while also leaving the wearer's hands free.


Innovations

Casio's innovations include the world's first entirely electric compact calculator, introduced in 1957, and the first graphing calculator in 1985. Casio also played a pioneering role in the development of digital watches, releasing the world's first watch with a full LCD display in 1974.


Our Mission

Casio aims to provide products that are innovative, reliable, and of high quality to meet the diverse needs of our customers. We continue to develop new technologies and products to enhance the convenience and quality of life for people around the world.
